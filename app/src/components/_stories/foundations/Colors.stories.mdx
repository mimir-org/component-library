# Colors

## Overview

Mimir and Tyle uses several colors to help users identify statuses, actions, and navigate the application in general.
Below you will find an overview of all the colors available in the design system, and that are consumable by the developers.

### Concept

As of 12.05.23 the color structure loosely follows material design 3's guidelines.
Read more about the color roles and intention behind the different color roles at
[Material Design 3 Color Roles](https://m3.material.io/styles/color/the-color-system/color-roles).

#### Example

Accent colors: Primary, secondary, and tertiary roles follow the pattern of a 4-color group.

Let's take the secondary color as an example:

- Secondary base color
- On-secondary is applied to content (icons, text, etc.) that sits on top of secondary
- Secondary container is applied to elements needing less emphasis than secondary
- On-secondary container is applied to content (icons, text, etc.) that sits on top of primary secondary
